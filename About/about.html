About cyberEdge

CyberEdge is a simple static website created to educate people about modern cyber threats that mostly recorded in 2026.

Purpose & Objectives

Purpose: The main purpose of this website is to help internet users understand the new types of cyber threats in 2026 and learn how to stay safe online.

Objectives:

  • To explain modern and most recent cyber threats in brief
  • To provide practical safety instructions
  • To increase awareness about online security among people

Why Is This Matter?

In 2026, cyber crimes like AI scams and deepfakes are increasing rapidly. Many people do not know how to protect themselves.

This website aims to solve this problem by giving basic but important information.
